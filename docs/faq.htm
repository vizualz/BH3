TOP FAQ’s

 

 

Q: Does Boardhunt sell boards and ship them?

A: Nope, we just showcase them so buyers and sellers can work it out.

 

Q: Does Boardhunt charge for showcasing or searching for boards?

A: Nope, we want to make this easy for you.

 

Q: Does Boardhunt make money of each board sold?

A: Nope, that’s not our style.

 

Q: Who can showcase boards for sale on Boardhunt?

A: Anyone who wants to turn boards into cash (business or non-business users)

 

Q: Does this site contain new and used boards?

A: Yes
